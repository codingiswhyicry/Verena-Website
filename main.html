meet Verena.

Verena is an app designed to keep you and other members of the LGBTQ+ community safe from hate crimes, abuse, and bullying. the app works as your personal security system, to make sure you get home safe.

your security comes first.

from the ground up, Verena is designed to keep you safe. the app stores all of your information locally, so nothing gets sent to 3rd party servers. your information is also saved in Keychain, an Apple API which encrypts your information, and will not be shared with anyone besides you. in addition, you're required to have a strong password and username. without, no one can get access to the app, or your info.
